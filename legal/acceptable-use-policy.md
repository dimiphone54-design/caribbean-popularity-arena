## 1. Purpose

1.1 This Acceptable Use Policy ("**AUP**") defines permitted and prohibited uses of the Platform, its APIs, interfaces, software, and infrastructure.

1.2 This AUP supplements the Terms and Conditions and Community Guidelines.

1.3 Violations may result in suspension, termination, legal action, and cooperation with authorities.

## 2. Lawful Use Requirements

2.1 You must use the Platform only for lawful purposes and in compliance with applicable local, national, and international laws.

2.2 You must not use the Platform to facilitate illegal activity, including fraud, harassment, exploitation, unauthorized surveillance, or unlawful solicitation.

2.3 You are responsible for determining whether your use is lawful in your jurisdiction.

---

## 3. Acceptable Uses

3.1 Permitted uses include:

(a) creating and maintaining lawful member or creator profiles;  
(b) participating in community engagement, voting, and showcase features as designed;  
(c) sharing content you have rights to share;  
(d) communicating respectfully where communication features are enabled;  
(e) purchasing disclosed digital access features through authorized payment flows;  
(f) reporting violations and cooperating with safety reviews.

## 4. Security Restrictions

4.1 You must not attempt to bypass authentication, access controls, encryption, or security measures.

4.2 You must not probe, scan, or test vulnerabilities without prior written authorization from [SECURITY@EXAMPLE.COM].

4.3 You must not introduce malware, viruses, worms, or harmful code.

4.4 You must not attempt to gain unauthorized access to accounts, servers, networks, or data.

## 5. Automated Abuse Restrictions

5.1 Automated scripts, bots, crawlers, or tools may not be used to interact with the Platform except through expressly authorized APIs or written permission.

5.2 Prohibited automation includes vote bots, fake engagement, mass account creation, and spam messaging.

5.3 Rate limits and technical barriers must not be circumvented.

---

## 6. Scraping Restrictions

6.1 Scraping, crawling, or systematic extraction of Platform content, profiles, rankings, or metadata is prohibited without prior written consent.

6.2 This includes use of browser automation to harvest data at scale.

---

## 7. Data Harvesting Restrictions

7.1 You must not collect personal data of other users beyond what the Platform intentionally displays for lawful interaction.

7.2 You must not compile user lists for unsolicited marketing, doxing, or resale.

7.3 Export of Platform data through unauthorized means is prohibited.

## 8. Bot Restrictions

8.1 Bots may not impersonate humans, manipulate rankings, create inauthentic engagement, or evade enforcement.

8.2 Authorized integrations must comply with technical documentation and security requirements when published.
