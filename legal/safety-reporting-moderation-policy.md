## 1. Purpose

1.1 This Safety, Reporting and Moderation Policy ("**Policy**") describes how users and creators can report concerns and how the Company investigates, moderates, and enforces safety standards.

1.2 This Policy supplements the Community Guidelines, Terms and Conditions, and Privacy Policy.

1.3 **If you are in immediate danger, contact local emergency services first.**

---

## 2. User Reporting Tools

2.1 Members may report profiles, content, messages, votes, or conduct through in-Platform reporting controls where available.

2.2 Reports may also be submitted to [SAFETY@EXAMPLE.COM] with:

- reporter account username or email;  
- reported account or content link;  
- date and description of incident;  
- screenshots or evidence where available.

2.3 Anonymous reports may be accepted where feasible, though follow-up may be limited.

---

## 3. Creator Reporting Tools

3.1 Creators may report abusive members, impersonation, harassment, fraud attempts, or policy violations using the same reporting channels.

3.2 Creators should report vote manipulation, fake accounts, and threats targeting their showcase or communications.

---

## 4. Abuse Reporting

4.1 Report physical threats, coercion, extortion, blackmail, stalking, or exploitation promptly.

4.2 We prioritize reports involving imminent harm, minors, non-consensual content, and credible threats.

---

## 5. Harassment Reporting

5.1 Report repeated unwanted contact, sexual harassment, bullying, or targeted hate even if individual messages appear minor in isolation.

5.2 Include pattern evidence where possible.

---

## 6. Fraud Reporting

6.1 Report scams, fake payment requests, phishing links, investment fraud, romance fraud, and impersonation of Company staff or payment processors.

6.2 Do not send money or sensitive data to unverified third parties claiming Platform affiliation.

---

## 7. Investigation Procedures

7.1 Reports are triaged by severity, credibility, and repeat offender history.

7.2 Investigations may review account data, content, metadata, payment records, and communication logs as permitted by law and policy.

7.3 We may contact involved parties for additional information.

7.4 Investigation timelines vary. We do not guarantee outcomes or disclosure of actions taken against other users.

---

## 8. Moderation Authority

8.1 The Company has sole discretion to remove content, restrict features, suspend accounts, or terminate access to enforce safety and policy compliance, except where limited by law.

8.2 Moderation may be performed by automated systems and trained personnel.

8.3 Automated decisions may be reviewed upon request where required by applicable law.

---

## 9. Emergency Actions

9.1 We may take immediate action—including account suspension, content removal, IP blocks, and payment holds—where necessary to mitigate credible risk.

9.2 Emergency actions may occur without prior notice.

9.3 We may preserve records and refer matters to law enforcement where appropriate.

---

## 10. Account Restrictions

10.1 Restrictions may include:

(a) messaging limits;  
(b) voting limits;  
(c) profile visibility reduction;  
(d) temporary showcase removal;  
(e) payment feature suspension.

10.2 Restrictions may escalate to permanent termination for serious or repeated violations.

---

## 11. Safety Enforcement Procedures

11.1 Enforcement follows a proportionality principle: warnings, restrictions, suspension, termination, and external referral.

11.2 Serious violations may skip preliminary warnings.

11.3 Users may appeal certain actions as described in the Community Guidelines.

---

## 12. Platform Cooperation Requirements

12.1 Users must cooperate with safety investigations and must not retaliate against reporters.

12.2 Users must not submit false reports to harm others.

12.3 We cooperate with lawful requests from regulators and law enforcement in accordance with applicable law and valid legal process.

12.4 Preservation requests and emergency disclosure procedures should be documented by counsel.

---

## 13. Off-Platform Conduct

13.1 Conduct arising from Platform introductions may be considered where it violates Platform policies or poses ongoing Platform risk, subject to applicable law and evidentiary standards.

13.2 Users are responsible for personal safety when meeting others offline.

---

## 14. Creator and Member Safety Recommendations

14.1 Protect personal information until trust is established.

14.2 Use in-Platform blocking and reporting tools.

14.3 Avoid sharing financial details or sending money based on online promises.

14.4 Meet in public places if choosing to meet offline and inform a trusted contact.
